I relocated my family to Boulder from our hometown, Carbondale, last summer so I could attend classes at Galvanize. This is the view of Mount Sopris from a trail on Red Hill with our hometown below.

I have a strong background in art and for ten years owned a sign making company in Santa Cruz, California. I have done everything from fine art, murals, tattoos, portraits, logos and label designs for products. I also worked for the County of Santa Cruz designing web pages, where I first developed an interest in programming.

I am so excited to have the opportunity to learn and grow with the creative, amazing and talented people at Galvanize!
